My 7 Favourite Films

By Andrew Burke

I really like films. Here are 7 that really made an impact on my life.

  • Star Wars My father took me to see this when I was 6 and it changed my life!
  • Inside Out When I first heard about the idea for this film - the characters are emotions inside a young girl's head - I couldn't imagine how Pixar would be able to make a movie out of it. But they did! And it's a masterpiece.
  • Days of Heaven Lethbridge Alberta never looked so good! A classic film from the 70s, entirely shot at "magic hour" as the sun goes down. Elliptical and poetic and tragic.
  • The Matrix A lot of movies in the 90s tried to depict what "hacking" felt like, but The Matrix finally managed it - mostly by turning it into kung fu.
  • Miller's Crossing This under-appreciated Cohen Brothers film is a love letter to classic film noir, with lots of great hats, amazing performances, and its own unique sassy language.
  • Mad Max Fury Road This film is to be played at maximum volume.
  • Scott Pilgrim vs. the World I'm a little biased because I used to live in exactly the same part of Toronto, but I keep finding new things in this film every time I watch it - and I've watched it a lot.
